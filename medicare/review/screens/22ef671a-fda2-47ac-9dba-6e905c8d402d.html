HEAD INJURY
• 1. Stop any bleeding by firmly pressing a clean cloth on the wound. If the injury is serious, be careful not to move the person's head. .

• 2. If you suspect a skull fracture, do not apply direct pressure to the bleeding site, and do not remove any debris from the wound.

• 3. If the person is vomiting, to prevent choking, roll the person's head, neck, and body as one unit onto their side.

• 4. Apply ice packs to swollen areas.
